Partially Attended

    Swine flu t-shirts

    Wed Jul 29, 2009

    84 Words
    Tags:
    I'm sitting at home in London with a head cold. For the dew minutes it
    took me to check my temperature (37 thank you very much) I was
    wondering whether it might be the old swine flu, but no, just a normal
    head cold.

    Made me think, someone should make a t-shirt with a biometric sensor
    that checks on true wearers' temperature. No fever and it would say
    "don't worry it's only a cold". Fever and, well there are lots of
    things it could say.
